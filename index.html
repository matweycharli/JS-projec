BMW

BMW AG (Rus. B'M-We[2], an abbreviation for Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, german for "Bavarian Motor Works") is a German manufacturer of automobiles, motorcycles, engines, and bicycles. The chairman of the company from 2006 to 2015 was Norbert Reithofer, since May 2015 - Harald Kruger, and since July 18, 2019 - Oliver Zipse. The chief designer is Josef Kaban.[3] The company's motto is "Freude am Fahren", in German: "With pleasure behind the wheel". For English-speaking countries, the motto "The Ultimate Driving Machine" was also coined.
